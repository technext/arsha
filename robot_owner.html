Robot owner

For robot owner

Robot owners can offer their unused robots for rent via R|Robot:

  • A List your robot on R|Robot

    You know your robot best, select a model or specify your robot.

    • - Please send us a email with the following information
    • - Please define the amount to charge per day/ month/ operation hours* you must take care to put the exact hours in the R|Robot system. The user will be than charged monthly.
    • - Please define the time-frame when you robot is available
    • - If you want you can add a picture of your robot or video

  • B Accept the customer and ship it to the customer

    • You know your robot best and you don’t want to give it to somebody you don’t trust in. So it is your decision which user you accept for your robot.
    • After you have agreed on the terms you have to ship the robot to the use. User are happy to get the robot immediately.

  • C Receive the robot back

    The user will send you the robot back, you can decide if you want to offer it again via R|Robot or to use it on your own

  • D Earn mony and give access to your robot

    Your robot user has to pay an upfront fee and a monthly fee. you will get it immediately when the user is charged by R|Robots
