Frequently Asked Questions

  • Question: How is offering robots on R|Robots.

    We as R|Robbots offer you the service to rent a robot direct from us. We offer for example Boston Dynamics spot. R|Robots is a platform to bring owner of robots together with users. You can rent a robot from an owner If you want

  • Why should I rent my robot on R|Robot?

    We have the latest models and are direct in contact with leading-edge robot manufacturers. We take care about the paperwork, insurance, replacement, maintenance, and shipping. So you can focus on employing the robot for your trade show, your event, your workshop, or just to get acquainted with the robot.

  • Question: My robot is damaged, what to do now:

    Don’t worry, every robot rented from R|Robot is covered by an insurance. Just inform us we will take care about it.

  • Question: My robot is so great I want to take it forever:

    You can directly request an offer for this type of robot. We will forward this to the manufacturer and get back to you with a proposition.

  • Question: Why are R|Robot is doing that:

    We love robots and want to enable others to experience the kind of joy in interacting with the latest tech as we do. Robots will definitely be a part of our future, so everyone should get acquainted to them.

  • Question: Who is the owner of the robot?

    The owner of the robot is the basic owner, you rent it that means you are responsible for it and what happens with the robot during your rental time
